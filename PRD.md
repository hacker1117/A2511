# Product Requirement Document (PRD) - Z Technology Limited Website

## 1. Project Overview
- **Project Name**: Z Technology Limited Corporate Website
- **Goal**: Create a premium, English-language showcase for Z Technology Limited's two main business lines: Consumer Electronics and Car Audio.
- **Target Audience**: Global distributors, wholesalers, and tech-savvy end consumers.
- **Key Success Metric**: High-end visual appeal that matches the "Z-Axiom" and "Car Audio" product quality.

## 2. Company & Brand Identity
- **Name**: Z Technology Limited
- **Brand Attributes**: Innovation, Premium Quality, Versatility, Deep Bass (Audio focus).
- **Design Aesthetic**:
    - **Theme**: Premium Dark Mode or High-Contrast Tech style.
    - **Colors**: Deep blacks, metallic accents (silver/gold), vibrant highlights to match product LED features if any.
    - **Typography**: Modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Roboto, or a "Tech" display font).

## 3. Site Structure

### 3.1 Home Page
- **Hero Section**: High-impact visual introducing "Z Technology Limited".
    - *Copy*: "Innovating Audio & Power" (or similar).
- **Business Overview**: Brief split-screen or dual-card layout introducing the two main pillars:
    - Consumer Electronics (Z-Axiom)
    - Car Audio Systems
- **Footer**: Contact info, copyright, social links.

### 3.2 Product Page: Consumer Electronics (Z-Axiom)
- **Product Name**: Z-Axiom
- **Concept**: 2-in-1 Wireless Power Bank + Bluetooth Speaker.
- **Key Features**:
    - **Speakerless Sound Box**: Turn any surface into a speaker.
    - **MagSafe Power Bank**: 5000mAh capacity, magnetic attachment.
    - **Dual Functionality**: Sound + Power in one sleek device.
    - **Kickstarter Success**: Link to Kickstarter page for social proof.
- **Visuals**: Use high-quality assets (DSC*.jpg) showing the device in lifestyle settings and close-ups.
- **External Link**: Feature a "View on Kickstarter" button.

### 3.3 Product Page: Car Audio (Ultra-thin Subwoofer)
- **Product Name**: Z-Series Ultra-Thin Subwoofer (Placeholder Name)
- **Concept**: High-performance bass in a compact form factor.
- **Key Features**:
    - **Space Saving**: Ultra-thin underseat design (fits tight spaces).
    - **Deep Bass**: 10-inch Woofer, 600W Peak / 150W RMS Power.
    - **Future Aesthetic**: Integrated Spider LED Light (App controlled, Blue/Colorful).
    - **Easy Installation**: Plug-and-play.
    - **Durability**: Aluminum alloy body for heat dissipation.
- **Visuals**: Use processed "Clean" images (clean_subwoofer_*.png) with english overlays in HTML.

### 3.4 Contact / About Us
- **Content**:
    - Company vision.

## 4. Technical Requirements
- **Frontend**: Standard HTML/JS or Vite-based build.
- **Styling**: Vanilla CSS with modern features (CSS Variables, Flexbox/Grid).
- **Responsiveness**: Mobile-first design.
- **Performance**: Optimized image loading (WebP conversion recommended).
- **No Backend**: Static site, form submissions via `mailto:` or placeholder.
